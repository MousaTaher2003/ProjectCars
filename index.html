BMW

BMW is a German multinational automobile manufacturer and luxury car manufacturer based in Munich, Bavaria, Germany.

Founded in 1916, BMW is one of the world's largest automobile manufacturers, with over 20,000 employees in 12,000 locations around the world.

BMW is the second-largest manufacturer of luxury vehicles in the world, after Mercedes-Benz, and the third-largest manufacturer of luxury cars in North America.

BMW is a member of the Volkswagen Group and the German automobile industry consortium Daimler AG.
