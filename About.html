The original BMW M3 (E30/2S, E30/2SC) was designed as a road-going version of the race car. However, more than 5,000 cars required for Approval Group A have been sold.

The BMW M5 (E36/3S, E36/3SC) is a sports car produced by BMW since 2006. It is the first car to be produced in the E36/3S and E36/3SC variants.

The BMW M6 (E46/4S, E46/4SC) is a sports car produced by BMW since 2006. It is the first car to be produced in the E46/4S and E46/4SC variants.
